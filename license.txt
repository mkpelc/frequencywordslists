-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Lists of words divided by letter and sorted by frequency of usage.
Content:
- List consists of 26 files for each language.
- English and polish languages are available
- Each of the files contains words starting on one given letter and sorted by frequency of usage.
